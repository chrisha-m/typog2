TNVR

Trap-Neuter-Vaccinate-Return or TNVR is a method animal advocates initiate to control the population of community cats that cannot be adopted into homes because they cannot adapt well to domestication due to innate behaviors. Feral and free- roaming cats also have better chances of surviving outdoors if they are neutered.

WHAT IS KAPON(SPAY/NEUTER)?

Kapon is a medical procedure done on animals wherein their reproductive organs are removed. This procedure not only helps with population control but is also beneficial for the behavior and overall health of your pet.
